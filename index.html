About Us

Welcome to Dhubulia Vivekananda Yuva Mahamandal (VYM). We are a dedicated youth organization striving to implement the man-making and character-building ideas of Swami Vivekananda.

Our core philosophy revolves around the holistic development of young minds. We believe that proper guidance, structured learning, and dedication to societal welfare can transform individuals and, by extension, the nation.

Study Circle

Academic Excellence

We provide a collaborative environment for deep learning, from sciences to history, ensuring conceptual clarity and structured preparation for competitive examinations.

Character Building

Education is incomplete without values. Our study circles actively discuss the teachings of great thinkers to build moral fortitude and intellectual honesty.

Peer Support

Learn together, grow together. Our network fosters a healthy competitive spirit while ensuring no student is left behind in their educational journey.
